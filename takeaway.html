This series of diary entries is essentially a transcript of my conversations with myself. The capture of a voice in my head that is constantly talking about something. In the best manner of the time, you can take all this with you, take it away.

May, 31

I think it’s time to try out the daily diary with fixation my thoughts at the moment. The experience with my daughter’s life story shows that it can be interesting to come back and re-read it over time.

I’m 34 today. And I feel I’m in the place, where I should be. I feel peace. I feel confidence. I feel at home close to my wife. Obviously I have made the right choices at some points in my life to bring me here.

We are on the islands now, surrounded by water. While my wife sleeps, I sit on the beach. 6 AM and nobody around.

We are in the most beautiful place, but hey, here is the thought of the day: technology is developing rapidly. Who needs hundreds of your photos that AI can find or create in seconds? So there is no need for all this equipment in my backpack like a camera, another one, drone, lenses. It seemed that one day we would finally be able to live lightly. Why not now?

Here is another thought: If taking pictures or videos of your holiday is no longer important, then what is? What the photograph cannot convey, for example. Personal experience. Smells, emotions, tastes.

If technology is rushing forward, maybe we should go in the opposite direction. Make less technically perfect shots on film. Tell stories without ideal compositions. Allow ourselves to be less perfect?

In my opinion, AI cannot replace us in this way. Every technology is designed to be better than humans — to do everything faster, smoother, cleaner. And here we are — we have limitations. And that is where we can be a serious competitor. We are human, and our weaknesses are the reason why everyone follows us.
